Honey Glazed Turnips

Description:

Honey was used by Vikings to sweeten dishes. This simple recipe highlights the natural sweetness of turnips.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs turnips, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil the turnips until tender, about 20 minutes.
  2. Drain and return to the pot.
  3. Add honey, butter, and salt, and cook over low heat until glazed and golden.

Serve hot as a delightful side dish.
